About Me

In case you were wondering, it's pronounced "Victor"🤓

I'm a Game Designer & Programmer based in NYC who primarily works in Unity. I am currently studying at CUNY BA under City College's Gaming Pathways Department and Hunter's Comp-Sci Department. I've have gained much of my development experience from EGD's Game Studio Program.

I've worked on numerous projects of varying lengths and team sizes, from solo-projects & small teams to 100-student mock studios. I'm comfortable in both leading and supporting roles.

I love creating elegant experiences that are accessible and easy to pick-up, but hold enough complexity to keep things engaging. Adding juice and ending with a polished product is very important to me.

My favorite video game franchise is Pikmin! 🌱
